Event Rounds

Round 1: Initial Screening

Participants will present their projects and demonstrate feasibility, innovation, and impact.

  • Technical soundness and implementation.
  • Innovation and creativity.
  • Feasibility and practical application.
  • Clear and effective presentation.

Round 2: Final Evaluation

Shortlisted teams will provide detailed demonstrations based on project type.

  • Hardware projects: Working prototype or model.
  • Software projects: Functional system.
  • Integrated projects: Demonstration of integrated systems.
  • Evaluated based on technical depth, impact, and scalability.
